FAQ

Online coaching


How long does the coaching last?

In order to optimally achieve your goals, we always recommend a duration of 3 months. Your body needs some time to adjust to your new lifestyle. If you would like to further expand your goals, an extension to 6 or 12 months is easily possible. Would you like to convince yourself first? No problem, if you are interested you are welcome to arrange a trial month with us!

How do I log on?

Registering for Steven's fitness program is super easy. First, answer the questions in our questionnaire. This will show your interest in the program and give us a rough idea of ​​what your goals are. You will then receive a call from Steven or one of our team members. In a personal conversation, we will find out more about your current fitness level, your personal goals, & you have the opportunity to find out more about the program and of course ask your questions. After the conversation, you will receive an offer and a payment link and can work on your personal goals together with Steven

Do I need additional training equipment?

First and foremost, you need motivation and willpower! The program can be used without any additional fitness equipment. Unless you would like to. The program is an individual fitness program with closeness to Steven. So if you prefer to train with weights etc., then feel free to mention this. We create a training plan that is individually tailored to you in order to optimally achieve your goals.


Will I be personally coached by Steven?

Yes, Steven is at your side as your personal fitness coach with advice and support during this time. He regularly adapts the training content and motivates you to achieve your goals!


Which devices can I use the app on?

The app can be used via common operating systems such as IOS and Android. No special device requirements are necessary.
